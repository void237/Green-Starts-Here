Basic Information

A home energy audit evaluates energy usage and identifies areas for improvement, such as inadequate insulation or air leaks.

Tax Rebate Information

Some utility companies offer rebates or incentives for conducting a home energy audit and making energy-efficient upgrades.

Cost

The cost of a home energy audit varies but typically ranges from $200 to $600. The cost of insulation installation depends on factors such as the size of the home and the type of insulation used.

Clean/Green Energy Practices

Improving insulation and sealing air leaks can reduce energy waste, lower heating and cooling costs, and improve indoor comfort while reducing carbon emissions.
