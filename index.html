The Purpose

Our purpose is to help make a greener world. Giving tips on making a greener home, information on fossil fuels, and greenhouse gases. Our environment is essential for our survival and the rest of life on Earth. We must do our best to protect it. We have many tips and ideas on how you can help make our world a greener place. Solar

Solar Power is power obtained by harnessing the energy of the sun's rays.

Wind

Wind Power is power obtained by harnessing the energy of the wind.

Hydro

Hydropower, also known as water power, is the use of falling or fast-running water to produce electricity or to power machines. This is achieved by converting the gravitational potential or kinetic energy of a water source to produce power.

Frequently Asked Questions

There are many questions about green energy, green living, and global warming and we are here to answer them!

  • What is global warming?

    Global warming is when the whole world starts to become warmer. While the worlds temperature has changed over millions of years, the worlds overall temperature is increasing at an alarming rate due to human activity and many living things are not able to adape fast enough and are starting to die off.

  • What is the difference between global warming and climate change?

    Global warming mainly refers to the recent increase in temprature of the Earth, while climate change refers to the changes in climate, which includes things like the weather.

  • What exactly is "green living?"

    "Green living" is living in a way in which you waste less. Whether it is by purchasing products that are made in ways that produce less waste, recycling, or anything other way.
